What is energy poverty?
Energy poverty has many different definitions, but it can be best described as a household who spends more than 10% of their income on energy bills, or a household that has to restrict electricity usage to afford the electricity bill.
what's energy poverty
Energy poverty occurs when a household is unable to afford a level of energy services necessary for its social and material needs.
What causes energy poverty?
Energy poverty is caused by inefficient buildings and appliances, high energy expenditure, and sometimes low household incomes.
